Our Story

Building the world's largest community of flower enthusiasts, florists, and growers — one bloom at a time.

OUR MISSION

Connecting People Through Flowers

Flower Ecosystem was born from a simple idea: flowers bring joy, and that joy should be shared. We started as a small forum for gardeners in 2024 and grew into a global platform serving over 89,000 members across 40+ countries.

Our mission is to make flowers accessible to everyone — whether you're buying your first bouquet, starting a floristry business, or growing roses in your backyard. We believe that the flower community is one of the most supportive and passionate communities in the world, and we're honored to be its home.

OUR JOURNEY

How We Grew

From a small idea to a global community

January 2024

The Seed Is Planted

Three friends with a shared love for flowers launched Flower Ecosystem as a simple online forum for gardeners.

June 2024

Marketplace Launch

Added our marketplace feature, connecting local florists with flower lovers across the country.

December 2024

10K Members

Reached our first 10,000 members. Launched the Learning Center with floristry courses and video tutorials.

April 2025

Global Expansion

Expanded to 20+ countries. Launched multi-currency support and international shipping options.

September 2025

AI Flower Scanner

Introduced our AI-powered flower identification tool, helping members identify any flower with a simple photo.

January 2026

50K Members

Celebrated 50,000 members and hosted our first international flower exhibition.

June 2026

89K & Growing

Today we serve 89,000+ members in 40+ countries, with 500+ events hosted and counting.
