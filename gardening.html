    Gardening Tips

    Quick advice to keep your garden thriving

    Watering Wisdom

    Water deeply in the morning to reduce evaporation. Most flowers need 1-2 inches of water per week. Always check soil moisture 2 inches deep before watering.

    Sunlight Guide

    Full sun = 6+ hours daily. Partial shade = 3-6 hours. Match plants to your garden's light conditions for best results.

    Pest Prevention

    Inspect plants weekly for pests. Companion planting with marigolds and herbs naturally deters many common garden pests.

    Soil Health

    Add compost annually to improve soil structure. Healthy soil grows healthy plants — test pH yearly and amend as needed.
